What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser by websites. They help the website recognise you on return visits, remember your preferences, and provide a better user experience.

Cookies come in two main types: session cookies (temporary cookies deleted when you close the browser) and persistent cookies (stored on your device for a defined period).

How to Manage Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. You can disable cookies entirely, allow only certain types, or delete existing cookies. However, disabling essential cookies may cause some features of the website to not function correctly.

How to access cookie settings in major browsers:

Google ChromeSettings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
Mozilla FirefoxSettings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
Microsoft EdgeSettings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies
SafariPreferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
